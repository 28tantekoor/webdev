Best Ice Cream in Southern Utah!

Cosmic Scoop is an ice cream shop built for people who believe dessert should be colorful, cold, and a little dramatic. We sell handmade ice cream, thick milkshakes, waffle cones, and sundaes.

Our Story

Cosmic Scoop was started by Markus Thompson in 2014 after he tried to name every flavor after something in space. His first cart only had vanilla, chocolate, and strawberry, but he called them Galaxy Vanilla, Black Hole Brownie, and Starlight Strawberry anyway.
